# TideMark Brand Colors

## Primary Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **TideMark Orange** | `#FF6B2B` | Primary brand color, stake body, logo accent |
| **Deep Ocean** | `#1A3A4A` | Text, headers, professional contexts |
| **Seafoam** | `#2BBFA0` | Secondary accent, highlights, links |
| **Sand** | `#F5F0E8` | Backgrounds, light surfaces |
| **Coral Red** | `#E8453C` | Target collar color, alerts, CTAs |

## Field Visibility Colors

These are functional colors for the physical product — chosen for visibility on job sites, not aesthetics.

| Part | Color | Hex | Reason |
|------|-------|-----|--------|
| Tower body | Safety Orange | `#FF6B2B` | OSHA-adjacent visibility, stands out against dirt/concrete/grass |
| Target collar | Signal Red | `#E8453C` | Contrasts against orange tower for photo readability |
| Alternative collar | White | `#FFFFFF` | High contrast option for very bright/dusty sites |
| Cap | Match tower | `#FF6B2B` | Visual unity |

## Typography

| Context | Font | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Logo / product name | Inter or DM Sans | Bold (700) |
| Headers | Inter | Semi-bold (600) |
| Body text | Inter | Regular (400) |
| Technical / measurements | JetBrains Mono or similar monospace | Regular (400) |

## Logo Usage

The TideMark logo consists of the wordmark "TideMark" with a subtle wave element integrated into the "T" or as an underline element.

- Primary: Deep Ocean on Sand/White background
- Reversed: White on Deep Ocean background
- Icon only: Wave mark in TideMark Orange (for small applications, favicons)

## Embossed Branding on Stakes

For the physical product, branding is embossed (raised or recessed) on the rear face of the tower:

```
TIDEMARK
─────────
OASISENGINEERING.COM
```

- Font: Bold sans-serif, minimum 4mm cap height for legibility
- Depth: 0.6mm emboss/deboss for print clarity
- Location: Rear face of tower, centered, positioned between graduation marks
