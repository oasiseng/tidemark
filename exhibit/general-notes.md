## Notes on Customization

- **Flood zone projects:** Add a note specifying the BFE and the required freeboard per the local floodplain ordinance. Example: "PER [JURISDICTION] FLOODPLAIN MANAGEMENT ORDINANCE, THE LOWEST FLOOR OF THE STRUCTURE SHALL BE AT OR ABOVE [BFE + FREEBOARD]' NAVD88."

- **Zone A (no published BFE):** Add: "THE PROJECT PARCEL IS LOCATED WITHIN FEMA FLOOD ZONE A, WHICH DOES NOT HAVE A PUBLISHED BASE FLOOD ELEVATION. THE COMMUNITY'S FLOODPLAIN ORDINANCE MAY REQUIRE A BFE DETERMINATION BY A LICENSED PROFESSIONAL. THIS EXHIBIT ADDRESSES FFE RELATIVE TO OBSERVED FIELD REFERENCES AND DOES NOT ESTABLISH BFE."

- **Remote assessments (no site visit):** Add: "ELEVATIONS SHOWN ARE BASED ON PUBLICLY AVAILABLE [COUNTY/CITY] GIS CONTOUR DATA AND CLIENT-PROVIDED SITE PHOTOGRAPHS. NO FIELD SURVEY OR DIRECT ELEVATION MEASUREMENT WAS PERFORMED BY THE ENGINEER."

- **TideMark kit included:** Add: "A TIDEMARK FIELD REFERENCE STAKE KIT HAS BEEN PROVIDED WITH THIS EXHIBIT. SEE ENCLOSED FIELD GUIDE FOR USAGE INSTRUCTIONS. THE TIDEMARK STAKE IS A REFERENCE TOOL AND DOES NOT REPLACE THE CONTRACTOR'S RESPONSIBILITY FOR INDEPENDENT ELEVATION VERIFICATION."
